In this chapter we are going to look at how to control where each element sits on a page and how to create attractive page layouts. This involves learning about how designing for a screen can be different to designing for other mediums (such as print). In this chapter we will: ● Explore different ways to position elements using normal flow, relative positioning, absolute positioning and floats. ● Discover how various devices have different screen sizes and resolution, and how this affects the design process. ● Learn the difference between fixed width and liquid layouts, and how they are created. ● Find out how designers use grids to make their page designs look more professional
